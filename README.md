# Jper - Japanese Kana Games

Feeling nervous about the various Japanese alphabets and the thought of memorizing them all?

Don't worry! Jper: Japanese Kana Games is here to transform your learning journey from a daunting task into an exciting and effortless experience.

Whether you are a complete beginner or a kid starting to explore a new language, Jper provides the perfect foundation for mastering the Japanese alphabet through listening and play.

[!jper_image_1](jper_image_1)

## 🎮 LEARN THROUGH PLAY

Say goodbye to boring rote memorization! Jper uses audio-based interactive gameplay to help you master both Hiragana and Katakana. By matching sounds to characters, your brain retains the alphabet faster and more naturally.

## 🌟 KEY FEATURES:

- **9 Progressive Levels**: Master Hiragana and Katakana step-by-step.

- **The "Jper Level" Challenge**: A special memory-based "Boss" mode where you must remember and repeat audio sequences before the timer runs out.

- **Audio-Visual Feedback**: Every character comes with high-quality sound samples to help you perfect your listening skills and pronunciation.

- **Clean and Simple UI**: A distraction-free interface designed specifically for children and beginners to focus on learning.

- **Level Progression System**: Earn your way through the levels by completing each set correctly to unlock the next challenge.

- **Debug Mode**.
